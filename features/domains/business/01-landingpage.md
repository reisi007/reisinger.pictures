# Technical Concept: B2B Landingpage

## 1. Value Proposition (USP)
Focus heavily on the "Reisinger Foto Portal" (`portal.reisinger.pictures`).
- **Speed:** Real-time delivery during the event.
- **Infrastructure:** IPTC-metadata injection, direct downloads for press teams, custom watermarks for guests.
- **Reliability:** Proven track record in high-pressure environments (Sports: LASK, Black Wings, ÖFB).

## 2. Content Structure (`src/content/areas/business.mdx`)
- **Hero Section:** Clear B2B messaging ("Echtzeit Content für PR & Events").
- **Services:**
    - Event-Reportage (Corporate Events, Congresses).
    - Corporate Portraits (Headshots, Team photos).
    - Live-Content API (The Portal).
- **Social Proof:** Use high-profile sports imagery as background/proof. *Crucial:* Do not use "small" B2C or minor B2B testimonials here to protect the high-end framing.
- **CTA:** Link to the new B2B Custom Pricing Builder.
